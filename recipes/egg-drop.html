Egg Drop Soup

Tasty homemade Egg Drop soup.


Ingredients

  • 6 c. chicken broth
  • 1/4 tsp. ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp. low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp. cornstarch
  • 1 tsp. sesame oil
  • 4 eggs, well beaten
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp. thinly sliced green onions, for garnish

Directions

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, bring broth and ginger to boil. Whisk soy sauce and cornstarch together then whisk into broth. Let boil to thicken slightly, 3 minutes then remove from heat and add sesame oil.
  2. Give the broth a big stir, then, in a slow stream, pour in eggs (do not stir again). Season with salt and pepper. Serve with green onions as garnish.
