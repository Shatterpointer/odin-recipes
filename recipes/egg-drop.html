Egg Drop Soup

Tasty homemade Egg Drop soup.


Ingredients

  • 6 c. chicken broth
  • 1/4 tsp. ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp. low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp. cornstarch
  • 1 tsp. sesame oil
  • 4 eggs, well beaten
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp. thinly sliced green onions, for garnish
