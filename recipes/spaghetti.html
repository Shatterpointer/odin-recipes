Spaghetti

Basic spaghetti with pasta sauce and parmesean cheese.


Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Water
  • Pasta sauce
  • Salt
  • Parmesean cheese

Directions

  1. Fill a pot 3/4 full with the appropriate amount of water, as designated by the spaghetti package.
  2. Add a tablespoon of salt and boil the water.
  3. Stir the noodles into the water.
  4. Cook according to spaghetti package, stirring frequently.
  5. Drain water and add pasta sauce and parmesean cheese.
