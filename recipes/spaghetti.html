Spaghetti

Basic spaghetti with pasta sauce and parmesean cheese.

Ingredients

    Spaghetti noodles
    Water
    Pasta sauce
    Salt
    Parmesean cheese

Steps

    Fill a pot 3/4 full with the appropriate amount of water, as designated by the spaghetti package.
    Add a tablespoon of salt and boil the water.
    Stir the noodles into the water.
    Cook according to spaghetti package, stirring frequently.
    Drain water and add pasta sauce and parmesean cheese.
